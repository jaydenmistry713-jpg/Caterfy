Our kitchen

We started with a market stall and a very heavy cheese board. Six years on, we cook for the days people remember.

Everything is made from scratch on the morning of your event — with produce from farms we can name, and usually visit on the way. We keep our bookings few so that every table gets the same unreasonable attention.
